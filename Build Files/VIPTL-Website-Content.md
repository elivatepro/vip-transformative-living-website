# CONTENT PRINCIPLES

## Voice & Tone

**Voice:** Confident, warm, direct, experienced, unpretentious

**Tone varies by context:**
- Homepage: Inspiring, possibility-focused
- About: Personal, story-driven
- Coaching: Clear, professional, value-focused
- Contact: Welcoming, low-pressure

## Writing Guidelines

1. **Lead with empathy** â€” Show you understand before offering solutions
2. **Be specific** â€” "25 years" not "many years"
3. **Use "you" language** â€” Make it about them, not about Coach Wayne
4. **Avoid jargon** â€” No coaching buzzwords without explanation
5. **Short paragraphs** â€” One idea per paragraph, especially on mobile
6. **Active voice** â€” "We help you" not "You will be helped"
7. **Clear CTAs** â€” Every section should guide toward an action
